About Catena Events

Every event is a chain of moments — we make sure none are weak links.

Catena Events is an Abuja-based catering and event management studio built for hosts who refuse to compromise. Whether it's an intimate housewarming or a five-hundred-guest corporate gala, our team plans the logistics, prepares the menu, and manages the room so you can simply be present.

Based at Emab Plaza, Wuse II, we work across Abuja and its surrounding areas, pairing meticulous planning with a kitchen that treats presentation as seriously as taste.
